Aircraft Maintenance Complex

Aircraft Maintenance Complex (AMC)
Asia Pacific & Polynesia

New technologies in aircraft development includes new and innovative designs for large capacity and flexible hangars that can serve varied aircraft service and control. While the hangar and rear offices are service areas, both sides of the hangar serve as control rooms if the building is located at an airstrip without flight control offices. The building's major material is locally sourced concrete and sheet metal over the large maintenance bay. The building is designed to withstand major weather conditions in the area, while also being large enough to shelter one medium sized aircraft or several small personal aircraft. The building is certified LEED Silver due to a sophisticated HVAC system that prioritizes service to different rooms, cools/heats the hangar through natural breezes and HVAC, non-mechanical drainage that does not runoff to soil or tarmac, and is specified to use eco-friendly materials.
