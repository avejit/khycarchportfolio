Moanalua Hillside Apartments
Moanalua, Oahu, HI

The proposal for the Moanalua Hillside Apartments is twofold: a new development to curb the growing lack of housing on Oahu, and to renovate the existing facilities for the area. The site is on the preexisting area of the Moanalua Apartment Complex, with the new buildings on previously unbuilt area. Four low-rise existing apartments will be demolished, replaced with a new 500+ unit towers, a new leasing office, recreation center, and other maintenance buildings. The clientele is aimed toward younger owners, who seek a place to live, work, and play, while being minutes to many attractions on and around the area of Moanalua.
