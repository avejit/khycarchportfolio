ASCC Multi-Purpose Center
Mapusaga, Tutuila, American Samoa

The American Samoa Community College (ASCC) is the beacon of secondary education for the residents of American Samoa. Therefore, the site of the ASCC Multi-Purpose Center is chosen for its centrality, and functions primarily for the school and public community. The wave roof concept responds to the sea-faring culture and to the nearby fale tele (meeting house). Approximately 2/3 of the space is flexible, reconfigurable for public functions or private corporate presentations. The remaining 1/3 of the space is dedicated for new campus functions. The project seeks a LEED Certification of Platinum , so the design incorporates certification features to achieve this.
