Haitang Bay Resort

Haitang Bay Resort
Sanya, Hainan Island

The unique site of Haitang Bay offers the opportunity to create a new type of residential resort community for Sanya and China. The development strategy concept will preserve the land and natural environment, the cultural identity, and the way of life of the region. Undergoing tremendous change, Hainan Island’s success is in growth of tourism, population and improved infrastructure, concentrated in Sanya and Haikou. This project seeks to further expand the growth of Sanya, expanding Sanya City and the currently popular Yalong Bay to the north and along the beautiful beaches of Haitang Bay and the east coast.
