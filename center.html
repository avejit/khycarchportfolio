New Jiangwan Culture Center

New Jiangwan Culture Center
Jiangwan, Shanghai, China

The New Jiangwan Town is a sustainable community model for China’s new development. The site, located in a central park, is designed to celebrate environmentally sustainable practice and the local community. The building’s 6,000sqm of exhibition, education, performance, and leisure facilities on two floors provides a fluid interaction between interior and exterior spaces. The horizontal layout of the building integrates with the exterior landscape and nature. Its dynamic spaces and flow encourages and stimulates public access and interaction. The blend of organic and inorganic materials contrast to celebrate the fusion of man and his environment.
