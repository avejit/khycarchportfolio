Haili Court Apartments

Haili Court Apartments
Hilo, Hawaii Island, HI

In this project, the site was a disused gas station in a neighborhood that serves as an automobile pass through corridor without any features. The project’s intent is to support the local community of Hilo. Its development would increase foot traffic, interest, and revitalize the local urban neighborhood. The basic design is residential apartments with loft-style bedrooms in each unit, combined with commercial space below. Ultimately, the purpose is twofold: to create more tourism and local value, with commercial space for local to serve tourists and locals, while the residences serve locals with the loft-space, rooftop access and shared gardens. The project is limited to only locals, and not for investment from non-residents.
