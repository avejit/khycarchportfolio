KOWA Boutique Hotel

KOWA Boutique Hotel
Waikiki, Oahu, HI

The KOWA Boutique Hotel is a wholly owned and constructed vacation destination, a part of the company’s vast portfolio of subsidiaries. The concept is quality over quantity; 18 hotel rooms & timeshares (9 with private Jacuzzis), with a full service ground floor restaurant and commercial sauna. It is a reconstruction of the previous Outrigger Waikiki Hotel, reinforcing the existing structural elements while refinishing with new KOWA textiles and finishing. Its reuse of the existing structure and upgrade of surfaces will revitalize the Kalakaua beach walk and is meant to entice more tourists from east asia to Oahu, while simultaneously showcasing KOWA construction finishes.
