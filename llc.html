Autoclaved Aerated Concrete Solutions LLC.
Milillani, Oahu, HI

Autoclaved Aerated Concrete (AAC) is a material that offers the same visual appearance as traditional CMU. When working with AAC as the primary structural element, designing buildings is very similar to traditional CMU blocks. We created designs that were purely functional, within a client’s budget and schedule. While not aesthetically pleasing buildings, we designed and delivered to our client a built environment was comfortable and fulfilled their desires and dreams. With this, we had ultimately fulfilled our project goals for the client and ourselves.
