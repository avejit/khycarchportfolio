Tianjin Shopping Plaza

Tianjin Shopping Space Design
Tianjin City, China

The development of Tianjin City required a new vibrant development that is in response to the modernization of the city and China. Departing previous Chinese-style layouts, the design incorporates modern trends and materials with tectonic form. The basic layout function conforms beyond the property lines, providing studies that show inclusive pedestrian flow from the surrounding neighborhoods. The concept is designed to be architecturally pleasing at both eye level and provide an atmosphere of modern and comfort. Ultimately, its purpose was to illustrate making the shopping experience more than just utilitarian; it is a welcoming experience reflecting modern China.
