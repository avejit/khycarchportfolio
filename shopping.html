Changshu Shopping District

Changshu Shopping District
Changshu, Suzhou, China

This design purpose is an entry into a local government competition. The building design covers the entire site, with a 6 floor shopping mall, office blocks, and a hotel/mix use tower. Because the site is located parallel to existing office blocks on the northern river way, the new office blocks are oriented north for continuity and for better ventilation (west-east). The hotel tower incorporates a curved surface to provide views for all guests. The blocks is designed with a sailboat shape, reminiscent to the Chinese character of “success”. The design is a vibrant commercial and business area; a mature commercial area to attract people locally and internationally.
