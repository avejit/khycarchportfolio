Panelized Steel Systems

Panelized Steel Systems, LLC.
Kaneohe, Oahu, HI

Panelized Steel provides a structural and elementally resistant system that incorporates all wall systems from plumbing to electrical to finishes. The possibilities of designing with this system are endless. Therefore, we created houses that were within a client’s budget and schedule, as well as being comfortable and fulfilled their desires and dreams. Working with their sister contracting company, the designed houses often conform to a more traditional “hawaii’ana” style, but were also reflective to the owner’s demand.
